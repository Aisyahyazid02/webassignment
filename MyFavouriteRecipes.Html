SPAGHETTI AGLIO OLIO

📍Ingrediants

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Mix herbs
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Seafood
  • Capsicum
  • Tomato
  • Parmesan

📍Prepare The Food

  1. Prepare The Food
  2. Boil spaghetti and drain
  3. Heat the olive oil and saute the garlic with the hollandaise until golden and wilted
  4. Add chilli flakes and mix herbs
  5. Then add shrimp and squid
  6. Chop The Capsicum and Tomato
  7. Add spaghetti followed by capsicum and tomato
  8. Pour a little spaghetti boiling water so that the ingredients are all mixed together during the process of frying the spaghetti
  9. Chill the spaghetti
  10. Lift and sprinkle parmesan
  11. Place on a plate and ready to serve, enjoy it
